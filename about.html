About me



Hi there! My name is Aykhan Kazimli and I am a 16-year-old student from Azerbaijan. I am so excited to welcome you to my online community and blog website! I have always been passionate about both computer science and environmental sustainability, and I created this platform as a way to share my ideas and insights with a wider audience. Through this website, I hope to inspire others to think about how they can use technology to create positive change in the world. Aside from my interest in tech and the environment, I have a background in music and have studied at a music school for five years. This has taught me the importance of discipline, practice, and creativity. In addition to my personal interests and hobbies, I have also participated in various academic and extracurricular activities. For example, I won a silver medal in a chess competition, which helped me develop my strategic thinking skills and ability to plan several moves ahead. I also won a silver medal in the Hackathon category at the STEAM International Festival, which showcased my ability to work collaboratively and creatively to solve complex problems. Furthermore, I am passionate about giving back to my community and have volunteered for various social projects. Through my experiences, I have developed my leadership and teamwork skills while also making a positive impact on the lives of others. Thank you for taking the time to visit my website, and I hope you enjoy reading my articles and learning more about my passions and experiences.
